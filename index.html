A Better Product for a Better Life

The AllKind Corporation was founded on the belief that there could always
be a better product, and whether we create our own product from scratch, or
develop off of an open source product, we are always working to bring you the better product that you deserve.

Helping the Tech Industry

We help the tech industry by giving back. Many of our
products have no ads, which means no source of income. Why do we do it
you may ask? We want to give back to the tech industry and consumers by being
a consumer friendly company, and that means our products will never have intrusive ads or frustrating microtransactions.
